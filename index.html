Engineering Course

During my preparatory classes, I took part in a project focused on modeling and analyzing a porous material for converting ambient humidity into electrical energy. This project enabled me to put theoretical concepts from my courses into practice particularly fluid mechanics and materials physics and to develop skills in numerical simulation and data visualization. Working on this project taught me how to structure a complete scientific approach: defining the problem, formulating a model, validating it through numerical tests, and interpreting the results. This experience strengthened my ability to solve complex problems and to collaborate effectively within a multidisciplinary team.﻿
